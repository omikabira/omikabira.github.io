Gurunanak College is situated on the Velachery, Chennai, India with a playground maintained by India Cements. The Guru Nanak College was established in the year 1971 as a Unit of Guru Nanak Educational Society to mark the 500th birthday of the great mentor, Shri Guru Nanak Devji, the founder of Sikh religion
